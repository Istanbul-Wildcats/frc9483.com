ABOUT

Istanbul Wildcats is a robotics team consisting of students from all over the world and mentors studying at distinguished universities in Turkey to share their interest and focus on robotics.

Our goal; To represent our country as the best FRC team in the world.

Our Mission; To reach all the young minds who want to participate in FRC competitions in our country and set an example for them with our skills in the field of robotics, while at the same time preparing for the future together in our world that develops at an incredible pace every day.

Our vision is to lead everyone we reach, both in Turkey and abroad, in the field of robotics and technology, while providing them with opportunities they could not even imagine, and to train the most equipped and prestigious engineers of the future.

Our team was established as an independent team in 2023. Our mentors, who competed in different teams when they were high school students in Turkey, united and founded Istanbul Wildcats in order to develop FRC Turkey by transferring their experiences to the next generation and to represent Turkey as a leading robotics team worldwide.

Our team currently consists of 5 mentors and 23 students. And at the end of each season, it opens applications to recruit new members for the team. Our team, which is not affiliated with any school or institution, sees this as an advantage and recruits members from all over Turkey and even the world.

As a team, we aim to see robotics competitions as sports and attract the attention of young people and enable them to discover the potential of robotics. We have discovered that electronic sports (E-sports) are an important platform to achieve this goal and we emphasize the importance of traditional sports competition continuing in a more respectful environment in the world of e-sports. Istanbul Wildcats aims to be a pioneer for an innovative future by emphasizing the importance of the robotics branch in this transformation and combining technology and sports. For this purpose, we continue our work within Istanbul Wildcats, an E-sports team, and represent them in the field of robotics.
